How do I work?

It's pretty simple:

First - add me to your Slack Team.
Second - write your welcome message or use my own one.
Third - I post a shoutout each time a new member joins the team.

Something like this:

GreetBotBOT
@channel hey everyone give a warm welcome to @peterpants! Good to have you here

Wanna get in touch?
