How much does it cost?

GreetBot is free to use. We are planning to add paid features in the future but that won't affect current users.

How can I customize GreetBot's shoutouts?

Just write settings in the direct message to GreetBot and you will be able to write a different shoutout.

This is how it works:

Justin

Settings

GreetBotBOT

Cool, cool. Here is what I can post to #general when a new member joins:

@channel hey everyone give a warm welcome to @new_user_name! Good to have you here 😊

What would you like me to do? You can type:

* Change to write a different message;
* Switch to send direct messages instead;
* Disable to stop me from making shoutouts;
* Nevermind to quit setttings.

Justin

Change

GreetBotBOT

Awesome possum! Please type the message that you would like me to use. You can use all text styles and emoji 😀

Pro tip: use the phrase @new_user_name as a placeholder for usernames. When new members join I'll replace it with their usernames instead 😎

Justin

hey people, give a warm welcome to @new_user_name! Great to have you on the team 😊

GreetBotBOT

Fantastic! Here is what I will post to #general when a new member joins the team:

hey people, give a warm welcome to @new_user_name! Great to have you on the team 😊

We are all set 👍 If you need to change anything just type settings at any time.

Can I greet new joiners privately in a direct message?

Yes! During the initial setup GreetBot will ask whether you’d like to send a public shoutout or greet new joiners privately:

GreetBotBOT

Yello! This is Greetbot, let's help your team welcome new members, shall we? Say yes to continue or no if you prefer to do it later.

Justin

Yes

GreetBotBOT

Excellent! How would you like me to welcome new members? You can type:

* Private to greet new members with a direct message
* Public to post a shoutout in a public channel

Pro tip: direct messages are more suitable in larger communities (less noisy) 💆, whereas public shoutouts work decidely better in smaller teams (more fun!) 🐣

Alternatively, if you already have GreetBot set to send public shoutouts you can go to settings and type switch to start sending direct messages instead.

This is how it works:

Can I ask GreetBot for help?

You can always type help and GreetBot will let you know what he can do for you.

This is how it works:

Justin

help

GreetBotBOT

Yello there, this is GreetBot 😊 I help you guys welcome new team members.

Currently I'm set to post a custom shoutout to #general whenever somone new joins your team:

@channel hey everyone give a warm welcome to @new_user_name! Good to have you here 😊

If you need me to change anything just type settings at any time.

You can read more on https://greetbot.io

Can I disable GreetBot?

Yes, you can disable shoutouts at any time. You just need to ask GreetBot for settings and use the keyword disable to turn everything off.

This is how it works:

Justin

Settings

GreetBotBOT

Cool, cool. Here is what I can post to #general when a new member joins:

@channel hey everyone give a warm welcome to @new_user_name! Good to have you here 😊

What would you like me to do? You can type:

* Change to write a different message;
* Switch to send direct messages instead;
* Disable to stop me from making shoutouts;
* Nevermind to quit setttings.

Justin

Disable

GreetBotBOT

Okay, got it. I'll stop posting shoutouts for now. Write again when you are ready to get going again. I'm happy to help 😀
